# Tic-Tac-Toe

Tic-tac-toe is a game for two players, Team DC VS Team MARVEL, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid.
The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game.
